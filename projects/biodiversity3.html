Enhancing Long-Term Biodiversity Monitoring through Transdisciplinary Approaches for Holistic Ecosystem protection

Ambition and Scope

BIODIVERSITY 3 is a Belmont Forum Collaborative Research Action currently in its co-construction phase. This means that everyone interested in the project can participate in co-construction (or “scoping”) workshops to contribute.

The main goal of BIODIVERSITY 3 is to complement and connect existing international long-term ecological research infrastructures at a broader transdisciplinary scale. It is designed to actively bridge the gap between the critical but short-term, co-designed projects fostered by the Belmont Forum and the establishment of durable, mechanistic long-term biodiversity monitoring programs.

Tip

You can receive information about the BIODIVERSITY 3 call and any capacity development events that may be taking place in advance of the official call launch, and to network with other interested parties by filling out interest form.
